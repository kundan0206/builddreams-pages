# BuildDreams Product Descriptions (Simple Sales Version)

This document is written for semi-technical sales use.
Use it to explain each product in simple words and quickly identify who to sell to.

## 1) Aspira
What it is:
A ready-made career platform that can be sold under your client brand (white-label).

Simple pitch:
"If a college or EdTech company wants its own placement and mentorship platform, Aspira gives them that without building software from scratch."

Who to sell to:
- Colleges and universities
- EdTech companies
- Training institutes
- Career services organizations

What problem it solves:
- Students, mentors, and employers are disconnected
- Building a platform in-house is expensive and slow
- Existing tools are not customizable

Key features to mention:
- Student, Mentor, and Employer portals
- White-label branding and custom domain
- Supports multiple revenue models (subscription, licensing, commission)

## Fast Sales Script (Use for all products)
Use this 5-step format in every call:

1. Problem:
"Today, teams struggle with [specific operational pain]."

2. Product:
"Our product [name] solves this by [simple core function]."

3. Outcome:
"This helps you reduce [time/cost/risk] and improve [speed/compliance/customer experience]."

4. Fit Check:
"If you currently manage [team/process/use case], this is a strong fit."

5. Next Step:
"We can show a live demo using one of your real workflows."

---

## Positioning Tip: VisionAI vs PartSight AI
Use this clear split to avoid confusion:
- PartSight AI: "Find and identify parts/components quickly."
- VisionAI: "Monitor broader visual events, defects, and compliance in real time."
