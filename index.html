07 · FAQ

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

Do you work with startups as well as enterprises?

Yes. Our custom-build arm works with Series A+ startups, while our SaaS suite is designed to scale from 5-seat teams to 50,000-seat enterprises — with the same schema.

How do you handle data residency for international clients?

We operate 6 deployment regions (Mumbai, Singapore, Frankfurt, Dublin, Virginia, Oregon). Enterprise customers can pin their tenant to a region or deploy into their own VPC.

Who owns the source code for custom builds?

You do — fully. On final invoice payment, we assign IP and hand over the complete repository, CI/CD pipelines, architecture docs and a recorded walkthrough.

What's the typical engagement timeline?

Discovery: 1-2 weeks. MVP: 6-10 weeks. Production-grade platforms: 12-20 weeks. Ongoing product partnerships: 6-month retainers with rolling scope.
