07 · FAQ

Frequently asked, honestly answered.

Do you work with startups as well as enterprises?

Yes. We work with teams that need custom software, workflow automation, or productized internal tools, whether the first rollout is small or already multi-team.

How do you handle data residency for international clients?

We discuss deployment needs during discovery and recommend the setup that matches the product, users, and compliance requirements.

Who owns the source code for custom builds?

Ownership and handover terms are defined per engagement, and we align on that before the build starts.

What's the typical engagement timeline?

Timelines depend on scope, integrations, and rollout goals. We estimate them after discovery instead of publishing generic promises here.
